# EPIC-003: Economy & Monetization Architecture

**Agent Owner:** `game-deconstructor`
**Dependencies:** EPIC-002

## Objective
Analyze how the competitors convert engaged players into paying users (Monetization) without causing churn.

## Acceptance Criteria (AC)
- Must identify at least 3 Faucets (Sources of soft/hard currency).
- Must identify at least 3 Sinks (Drains of soft/hard currency).
- Must evaluate the ad-integration strategy (e.g., Rewarded Video vs. Interstitials).
- Must analyze the Pricing Elasticity (e.g., cost of standard starter bundles).

## Definition of Done (DoD)
- Section starts with a BLUF.
- Sinks and Faucets are presented in a comparative Matrix/Table.
- Output is appended to `{OUTPUT_FILE}` under `## Part 3: Economy Sinks & Faucets`.
