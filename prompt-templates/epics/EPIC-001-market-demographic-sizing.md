# EPIC-001: Market & Demographic Sizing

**Agent Owner:** `market-researcher`
**Dependencies:** None

## Objective
Establish the Total Addressable Market (TAM), analyze regional saturation in SEA, and provide baseline Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) for Casual Match-3 games on HTML5 platforms.

## Acceptance Criteria (AC)
- Must include a clear estimate of the TAM for female gamers (18-35) in SEA.
- Must provide a breakdown of estimated CPI, ARPDAU, and D1/D7/D30 Retention metrics.
- Must differentiate between traditional App Store distribution and HTML5 Mini-App distribution costs.

## Definition of Done (DoD)
- Section starts with a BLUF.
- All metrics are presented in a Markdown table.
- Hallucinated or inferred metrics are tagged with `[ESTIMATE]`.
- Output is successfully appended to `{OUTPUT_FILE}` under the header `## Part 1: Market Feasibility`.
